Meditation

What is meditation?

the definiton of meditation by the oxford dictionary:

  1. the practice of thinking deeply in silence, especially for religious reasons or in order to make your mind calm
  2. meditation (on something) -> serious thoughts on a particular subject that someone writes down or speaks

Meditation as viewed in the buddhism

meditation can be viewed and defined in many different ways,
for the buddhists- meditation is following our higher purpose.
Essentialy that is a way of getting in touch with reality,
embrace whatever is presented infront of you,
and by doing so resistance is vanished(external and internal),
you start to flow with the natural stream of life -
and in some sense serving a bigger purpose, one that you are not aware of.
